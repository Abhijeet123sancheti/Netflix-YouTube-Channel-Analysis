# Netflix-YouTube-Channel-Analysis

## 📊 Data-Driven Insights: Netflix India YouTube Channel Analysis 🎬
        I analyzed the performance of Netflix India on YouTube — and the results 
        were fascinating! 🚀 With 11B+ total views, 137M+ likes, and 3M+ comments, the channel reflects
        massive audience engagement across India.
## 🔍 Key Insights:
       ▪ Wednesday receives the highest number of views
       ▪ 66% of views come from short videos
       ▪ Around 11 AM is the peak activity time
       ▪ 1,230+ videos crossed 1M views
       ▪ Tag count & title length have minimal impact on performance
       ▪ Videos posted closer to weekends show stronger engagement
##  🥇 Most Popular Video:
       Mighty Little Bheem | Bheem with a Beat
       Short duration (~3 mins), strong kid-friendly appeal, and smart tagging contributed to its massive success.
      📈 Strategic Takeaways:
       ✔ Focus on engagement metrics (likes, comments, shares)
       ✔ Leverage short-form content
       ✔ Post during peak hours
       ✔ Repurpose memorable scenes into shorts
       ✔ Collaborate with creators & influencers
       ✔ Promote trailers & behind-the-scenes content
